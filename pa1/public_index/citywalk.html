Universal CityWalk

The entertainment and shopping areas next to Universal Studios Orlando Parks & Resorts' amusement parks collectively go by the moniker of Universal CityWalk. CityWalk serves as a gateway from the parking lots to the amusement parks and was initially built as an addition to Universal's first park, Universal Studios Hollywood. Universal Orlando Resort in Florida, Universal Studios Japan in Osaka, Japan, and Universal Beijing Resort in Beijing, China all include CityWalk attractions.

Despite sharing certain tenants, CityWalk Hollywood and CityWalk Orlando have quite different architectural designs. CityWalk Orlando is nearly wholly modern in look, in contrast to CityWalk Hollywood, which features a classic modern combination of Hollywood.

This section includes a walking path directly to Universal's Islands of Adventure as well as the Universal Studios section of the park.

Universal also keeps a large portion of it's merchandise in this area. Some of the more popular products are from lands such as Toon Lagoon, Marvel Superhero Island, and The Wizarding World of Harry Potter.
