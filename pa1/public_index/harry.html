The Wizarding World of Harry Potter

At the Universal Studios Orlando Resort in Orlando, Florida, there is a themed section called The Wizarding World of Harry Potter that spans two theme parks, Islands of Adventure and Universal Studios Florida. The section has a Harry Potter media franchise theme and features adaptations of J. K. Rowling's books and film series. Using an exclusive license from Warner Bros. Entertainment, Universal Creative created The Wizarding World of Harry Potter. It has been voted a top vacation spot since it opened nearly a decade ago, and Universal does not plan to stop expanding this area of the park. This is no surprise due to the amount of revenue this land generates every year.

It is connected to Universal's Islands of Adventure and is accessible in the back of the park. It is also visible from Universal CityWalk.
